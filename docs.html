How To Use:

  1. The first step is to download 20x3.zip from here and save it in a memorable location. Your desktop or your Documents is the recommended location.
  2. Right click on 20x3.zip and press extract all.
  3. Select the desired location for the package to be stored in, then click extract. If you're unsure, just click extract.
  4. Navigate to the extracted file, and run 20x3.exe
  5. Bish Bash Bosh jobs a good'en, you've successfully downloaded and run the program. However, you must remember to do this every time you start your computer. To do this automatically, see below.

    6. Note: You may be given a warning when you first run 20x3.exe. Just ignore this, as 20x3 will not harm your computer.

Run 20x3 on Startup:

  1. Find the location of 20x3.exe, right click and select "Create shortcut". A file titled: "20x3.exe - Shortcut" will appear in the location of 20x3.exe.
  2. Press Windows + R on your keyboard and type "shell:startup". This will open a folder containing shortcuts for programs that launch at startup on your computer.
  3. Move the "20x3.exe - Shortcut" you made earlier into this startup folder.
  4. Reboot you computer and you should see a notification pop up when you log in, indicating that 20x3 has started.
